Busy Birthday

Today is my birthday, however, its been pretty busy with school. Unfortunately for me, I had to take a very hard midterm today which took up most of my mind. The midterm was in my basic engineering course on statistics. Additionally, I was up until 2 am last night studying for it as I had an exam yesterday that took up a lot of my study time. This morning I woke up at 7 am for my 8:30 am lab as well! Running on 5 hours of sleep and taking midterms on your birthday is pretty rough. However, now that my exams are over, I am glad I can relax and enjoy my birthday.

I turned 20 today! Which is crazy because I still feel like a teenager. It feels a little bitter sweet turning 20, as I always felt a little negative about growing up. However, I realize I am a year closer to reaching my goals!

I have planned to go to a sushi restaurant for my birthday, my favorite food! Also, I had given up dairy and meat for the first week of lent so sushi may be the only option. Regardless, I am excited.

My Sushi Order:

  • Cucumber Salad
  • Miso Soup
  • Rainbow Roll
  • Spicy Salmon Roll

Lent

In my religion, we celebrate lent to which we fast for 40 days from whatever we may choose

I've been brainstorming a list of things I am going to give up and add into my lent this year.

My Lent:

  • No meat and dairy during the first week, last week, and on fridays
  • No music, besides study music
  • No junk food
  • Give up 1 social media
  • Add more prayer

I chose these things because I know that it will take a lot of discipline to refrain from them. Without struggle, are we really fasting?

A friend once told me…

Lent is a Time for
Renewing Faith
Hope
and Love.
