Recipe 1

Category: Breakfast

Avocado Toast

Ingredients:

  • Sourdough bread
  • Avocado
  • Olive oil
  • Tomatoes
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions:

1.Toast slice of sourdough bread until golden brown.
2.While the bread is toasting, mash the avocado in a bowl until smooth.
3.Spread the mashed avocado evenly onto the toasted bread slice.
4.Drizzle olive oil over the avocado toast.
5.Slice the tomatoes and place them on top of the avocado.
6.Season with salt and pepper to taste.
7.Serve and enjoy!

Recipe 2

Category: Lunch

Sesame Ginger Chicken Salad

Ingredients:

  • Spring Mix
  • Baby Tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Marinated Grilled Chicken
  • Sesame Ginger Dressing

Instructions:

1.Wash and chop the spring mix, baby tomatoes, and cucumber.
2.Grill the marinated chicken until fully cooked.
3.Once the chicken is cooked, slice it into thin strips.
4.In a large bowl, combine the spring mix, baby tomatoes, cucumber, and grilled chicken strips.
5.Drizzle the sesame ginger dressing over the salad.
6.Toss the salad until all ingredients are evenly coated with the dressing.
7.Serve immediately and enjoy!

Recipe 3

Category: Dinner

Chickpea Pasta

Ingredients:

  • Banza Chickpea Pasta
  • Tomato Paste
  • Sundried Tomatoes
  • Spinach Pieces
  • Almond Milk
  • Parmesan Cheese

Instructions:

1.Boil a pot of water.
2.Add the Banza Chickpea Pasta to the boiling water and cook according to package instructions until al dente.
3.While the pasta is cooking, heat a pan over medium heat.
4.Add tomato paste to the pan and cook for 1-2 minutes.
5.Chop sundried tomatoes and add them to the pan with spinach pieces. Cook for another 2-3 minutes.
6.Drain the cooked pasta and add it to the pan with the tomato sauce and vegetables.
7.Add almond milk to the pan and stir until the sauce is well combined and heated through.
8.Serve the chickpea pasta hot, topped with Parmesan cheese.
