Lent

Feb 10, 2024

In my religion, we celebrate lent to which we fast for 40 days from whatever we may choose

I've been brainstorming a list of things I am going to give up and add into my lent this year.

My Lent:

  • No meat and dairy during the first week, last week, and on Fridays
  • No music, besides study music
  • No junk food
  • Give up 1 social media
  • Add more prayer

I chose these things because I know that it will take a lot of discipline to refrain from them. Without struggle, are we really fasting?

A friend once told me…

Lent is a Time for
Renewing Faith
Hope
and Love.
